Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Thank you for choosing GEOID STUDY CENTRE. We aim to provide the highest quality educational resources. Please read our policy regarding refunds and cancellations carefully before enrolling in our programs.

1. Course Enrollment Fees

Once a student enrolls in a course and payment is processed, the following conditions apply:

  • Full Refund: Eligible if requested within 48 hours of payment, provided the course materials have not been accessed or downloaded.
  • Partial Refund: If a cancellation is requested after 48 hours but before the start of the first session, a 20% administrative fee will be deducted.
  • No Refund: No refunds will be issued once the course has commenced or if digital access has been utilized.

2. Digital Products & Materials

Due to the nature of digital content, all sales of downloadable study materials, e-books, and recorded lectures are final and non-refundable once the download link has been generated or accessed.

3. Physical Goods

In cases where physical study modules or books are shipped:

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery only if the items are in their original, sealed condition.
  • The student is responsible for all return shipping costs.
  • Damaged items will be replaced at no extra cost if reported with proof (photo/video) within 24 hours of receipt.

4. Technical Issues

If you are unable to access our services due to technical failure on our end, we will first attempt to rectify the issue. If the issue persists for more than 7 working days, a full or pro-rata refund may be considered based on the duration of service loss.

5. Contact Us

For any questions related to refunds or cancellations, please reach out to our administration team at:

geoidstudycentre@gmail.com
